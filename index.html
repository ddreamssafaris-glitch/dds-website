Exploring Nairobi as a Local

4 Hours (Extendable) Walking Tour $42 per person ⭐ Best Seller

For those visiting Nairobi, there's more than just the typical safari. Kenya's vibrant capital offers a rich blend of history, modern culture, and local traditions best discovered on foot. Spend 4 hours walking the city as a local — taking in historic sites, tasting local flavours, and connecting with the heart of Kenya.

The Itinerary
1

09:00 — August 7th Memorial Park

Begin at the August 7th Memorial Park (Old American Embassy), site of the 1998 U.S. Embassy bombing. This peaceful garden with benches and commemorative plaques reflects Nairobi's resilience. A small on-site museum offers firsthand accounts of the event — a moving and important part of understanding the city's journey.

2

Kenyatta International Convention Centre (KICC)

A short walk to the iconic KICC — a 28-storey cylindrical tower and symbol of Kenya's ambition. Take the elevator to the rooftop observation deck for stunning 360° views of Nairobi's skyline, bustling streets, and the distant Ngong Hills. Perfect for photography.

3

Parliament Buildings & Jomo Kenyatta Mausoleum

Stroll past Kenya's Parliament Buildings and the Mzee Jomo Kenyatta Mausoleum — resting place of Kenya's founding father. Though not open to the public, the exterior is a deeply meaningful site. Nearby ministerial buildings add to the political and historical significance of this stretch.

4

The Holy Family Basilica

A short walk brings you to this stunning Roman Catholic church — soaring ceilings, modern architecture, and beautiful stained-glass windows. A quiet and reflective break from the city buzz, offering insight into the spiritual life of many Nairobians.

5

Lunchtime — K'Osewe (Ranalo Foods)

End your walk at K'Osewe on Moi Avenue — a beloved Nairobi institution where locals gather for authentic Kenyan cuisine. Sample ugali, sukuma wiki, nyama choma, and a glass of mala (fermented milk). The lively communal atmosphere is an experience in itself. Can be extended further on request.

Air Package Safari: Mara Plains

5 Days Fly-In Safari Masai Mara Price on Request

Skip the long road transfer and fly directly into the heart of the Masai Mara. This premium 5-day air package puts you in the wildlife action faster, staying at the intimate Zebra Plains Mara Camp for a deeply immersive experience.

Day-by-Day Itinerary
1

Arrival in Nairobi

Meet and greet at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport by your Distant Dreams representative. Transfer to Sarova Panafric Hotel for a comfortable overnight in the city.

2

Nairobi → Masai Mara by Air

Early breakfast at Panafric Hotel before transferring to Wilson Airport for your Air Kenya Safari Link flight into the Mara. Arrive in the wild by late morning. After settling in at Zebra Plains Mara Camp, enjoy an afternoon game drive across the sweeping savanna plains. Lunch and dinner included. Overnight at Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

3

Full Day in the Masai Mara

A full day of morning and afternoon game drives in one of Africa's greatest wildlife reserves. Track the Big Five, witness dramatic predator interactions, and photograph the golden savanna at its most spectacular. All meals included. Overnight at Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

4

More of the Wild Mara

Another full day of morning and afternoon game drives. The Masai Mara rewards repeat exploration — different corners of the reserve reveal new wildlife sightings each time. All meals included. Final overnight at Zebra Plains Mara Camp.

5

Mara → Nairobi → Departure

Board your Safari Link return flight from the Mara airstrip back to Nairobi. Transfer to Jomo Kenyatta International Airport for your onward departure flight, carrying home extraordinary memories of the wild.

Optional Extension: 6-night beach stay at White Sands Hotel, Mombasa — full board, return airport transfers. Available on request.

Kenya Unveiled Grand Safari

9 Days Multi-Park Masai Mara · Nakuru · Naivasha · Amboseli Price on Request

Your Kenyan adventure begins late evening and unfolds across nine extraordinary days — from the legendary Masai Mara to flamingo-pink Lake Nakuru, serene Lake Naivasha, the drama of Hell's Gate, and finally Amboseli with its elephants set against the backdrop of Mount Kilimanjaro.

Day-by-Day Itinerary
1

Arrival in Nairobi — Gateway to Adventure

Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (22:00hrs). A warm welcome from your Distant Dreams representative before transfer to the elegant EKA Hotel for a comfortable overnight rest.

2

Nairobi → Masai Mara — Wild Encounters Begin

After a hearty breakfast, embark on a scenic drive to the legendary Masai Mara National Reserve. Arrive at Little Zebra River Camp around lunchtime. After settling in, enjoy your first thrilling game drive across the vast golden plains searching for the Big Five. Return to camp for dinner under the African stars.

3

Masai Mara — A Symphony of Wildlife

A full day dedicated to the Mara's untamed beauty. Morning and afternoon game drives take you deeper into this world-renowned wilderness — colossal elephants, graceful giraffes, elusive big cats, and countless plains game. Your expert guide leads you to prime viewing spots for unparalleled photographic opportunities. Overnight at Little Zebra River Camp.

4

Lake Nakuru — A Flamingo-Pink Horizon

Bid farewell to the Mara and journey to captivating Lake Nakuru National Park. Arrive at Sarova Lion Hills around lunchtime. The afternoon brings an engaging game drive through diverse ecosystems — vibrant birdlife, rare Rothschild's giraffes, and both black and white rhinos. Overnight at Sarova Lion Hills.

5

Lake Naivasha — Lakeside Serenity

After breakfast, journey to the picturesque Lake Naivasha and check in to Enashipai Resort for lunch. The afternoon is spent on a serene boat ride across the lake, watching hippos wallow and fish eagles swoop in the golden afternoon light.

6

Hell's Gate — Active Exploration

An exhilarating morning bicycle tour through the dramatic landscapes of Hell's Gate National Park — towering cliffs, volcanic gorges, and wildlife roaming freely around you including zebras, giraffes, and gazelles. Return to Enashipai Resort for a well-deserved lunch and a relaxed afternoon by the lake.

7

Amboseli — Kilimanjaro's Majestic Backdrop

Early morning breakfast then a scenic drive to Amboseli National Park, famous for its large elephant herds and breathtaking views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Check in at Kibo Tented Camp before an afternoon game drive across the flat, dusty plains — iconic elephants photographed against Africa's highest peak.

8

Amboseli — Giants & Cultural Rhythms

A full day in Amboseli with extensive game drives. Beyond the wildlife, immerse yourself in the rich culture of the Maasai people with a visit to a local traditional village — learn their dances, traditions, and way of life. Return to Kibo Tented Camp for dinner under the stars.

9

Amboseli → Nairobi — Farewell & Fond Memories

Savour a final breakfast with Amboseli's landscape before checking out. Drive to Nairobi for a farewell lunch at the charming Court Yard Utamaduni Restaurant. Visit the Giraffe Centre for a final magical encounter — hand-feeding endangered Rothschild's giraffes. Transfer to JKIA for your onward journey, carrying extraordinary memories of Kenya.
